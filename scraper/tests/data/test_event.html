Bourbon Friday

Details

Join us at Techartista Central West End on Friday, April 17th, for Bourbon Friday, a weekly social gathering celebrating the St. Louis startup scene. Each week, we come together to share our stories, struggles, experiences, and, yes, a little bit of bourbon.

Mingle & Tastings // 4-7pm

Parking & Transportation
Street parking is available for Bourbon Friday.
You may also park in the church parking lot across the street from TechArtista.
We are also only a 1-mile Walk from Central West End Metro Station.
